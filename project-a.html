Digital Documentation of Byzantine Churches in Laconia

This project aims to digitally document and analyze more than one hundred Byzantine churches located in the Laconia region of southern Greece. The research combines photogrammetry-based 3D modeling with historical, architectural, and art historical analysis.

Selected 3D Models

These models represent part of the ongoing digital documentation of over one hundred Byzantine churches in the Laconia region. They are created using photogrammetry and are accessible interactively below.
